About

Planning with people, place, and the planet in mind.

With a background in urban design and community planning, I work at the intersection of policy, sustainability, and everyday life. My approach is collaborative: I listen first, map the realities on the ground, and build plans that neighborhoods actually want.

From transit corridors to pocket parks, I care about the small details that make a city feel alive — shade on a sidewalk, a safe crossing, a place to sit.
